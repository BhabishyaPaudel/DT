Our Mission

The Duia Trailblazers Academy is committed to setting the standard for outdoor leadership and safety in India. We provide aspiring guides, outdoor professionals, and enthusiasts with the practical skills and certifications needed to lead adventures responsibly and confidently.

Courses & Workshops

Canyoning Course

Master the techniques of canyon descent, from rope work and anchor systems to water safety and rescue.

Trek Leadership

Learn group management, advanced navigation, risk assessment, and emergency response for high-altitude treks.

Wilderness First Aid

Get certified in wilderness first aid and CPR, focusing on situation management in remote environments.

Our Story

Who We Are

Duia Trailblazers was born from a passion for the unexplored beauty of Meghalaya and a deep respect for its indigenous cultures. Our founder, Gerald Samuel Duia, envisioned a company that not only shared this magic with the world but also empowered the local communities and set a new standard for sustainable adventure.

Today, we are a team of certified local guides, expert instructors, and travel enthusiasts dedicated to crafting authentic experiences. The Duia Trailblazers Academy is our commitment to building capacity, ensuring safety, and fostering the next generation of outdoor leaders.
